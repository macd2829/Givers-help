🌸 Remembering Linda Grandmother (Maria Andras)

Maria Andras, known to everyone as Linda Grandmother, was the heart of our family and community. With her gentle hands she mended broken things, with her laughter she lifted heavy hearts. For 83 years she gave warmth, home-cooked meals, and endless wisdom.

After a brave journey, she gained her wings in early 2025. Her final wish was to support underprivileged children in her village and to create a small memorial library in her name. We are raising funds to fulfill her dream and give her legacy a beautiful home.

All donations will go toward: 🕊️ Memorial bench & garden, 📚 Children's library fund, 💊 Medical aid for elders (as she cared deeply). Every contribution, big or small, will be blessed in her memory.

🎯 Fundraising Goal: $12,500 ❤️ Raised so far: $7,750

Thanks to 143 kind souls — we are 62% closer to Linda's legacy library. Help us reach the finish line.
